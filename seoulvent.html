Job Opportunities

Full-time @ Permanent  Apr 2022—Present

For the most of the part, full-time job is known as the hardest job you can take, since employees must work from 9 to 5. (there are exceptions but the rule applies to most of the companies.) I recommend taking a spot in the Children-Education, College Professor, and jobs related to teaching. To teach the students, you need to have a strong background of the field that you're going to teach. You can prove that through professional diplomas. Here are more tips for tips and vlogs for a full-time employee in korea; 5 Helpful Websites, Websites for Indians, and Tips.

Part-Time @ Anywhere and Anytime  Jun 2022—Present

Part time job is the easiest way to earn money without extra portfolios or work experiences. In some cases, a company requires you to submit Korean-Proficiency Test Result. Most immigrants and even high school students living in Korea can make a profit through café, restaurant, manager, tutoring, staff, and etc; It's also important to familiarize oneself with the Korean work culture, which often emphasizes punctuality, respect for hierarchy, and a strong work ethic. Look out for the details above the description box!

Contract @ Flexible  May 2022—Present

Like Part-Time jobs, contractor is also prospective career foreigners can have. Independent Contractor is also increasing. If you want more secure spot, the Under-Goverment Contractor can be a good position. Temporary Contractor might also be suitable for your preference. More information: Tutor Contractor's Experience & Other jobs available. There are not as much as requiremnets as there are for the full-time jobs, but this job stil asks general Korean Proficiency level or work experiences.

Self-Employed @ Foreign CEO's guide and advice  Mar 2022—Present

If you are not a type of a person who want to work for other people, it is better to hire yourself. There are business ideas everywhere in Korea. You just need to know the right timing and the market.

Freelancer @ Jooble  May 2022—Present

Foreign freelancers in South Korea should demonstrate expertise in their field with a strong portfolio, possess effective communication skills (Korean proficiency is beneficial), and understand local cultural norms and business etiquette. They need a reliable internet connection, suitable workspace, and should be adept at self-promotion using social media and networking events. Flexible and comfortable spots are categorized as freelancing.

Remote/Telecommuter @ Glassdor  May 2022—Present

The mix of part time job and freelancing but without specific workplace is called Telecommuter. There are high popularity of foreign people who start social media to vlog their foreign life and try to earn the profit at the same time!

Tips For Resume & Interview

Choose The Field @ Top 10 Jobs For Foreigners In Korea  Aug 2022—Present

As we live in the fastest, developing, and revolutional time of the century, you need to consider what type of jobs are benefited the most. According to recent research, the most promising job sectors in South Korea in 2024 include technology and engineering, healthcare, education, finance and fintech, and manufacturing and industry. However, if you are already expert in specific field, it might be better to follow along the careers you are good at. In addition, there are some foreigners who try whole different route of careers just to build an experience.

Make Portfolio @ The Most Powerful Resume Template  Jul 2022—Present

Making a portfolio about your experiences and what you've achieved are especially IMPORTANT in Korean community because this country is based on the world of meritocracy. Korean Proficiency Test or the CERTIFICATE of your prospective career will make your resume more appealing. On the description above, I attatched the most powerful portfolio for foreigners.

Fill Out The Resume @ Full Guidance For Resume  Mar 2022—Present

Now, it is time to fill out your Resume! Resume is the only part of the application process that you can fully show your talent of the career path that you selected! If you already initiated the previous steps above, it will be easier to write the resume, since all you have to do is putting down the past achievements that you've gone through! Not to mention, Many Korean companies hope to see the cover letter to recognize your work-place attitude. Please find your teacher, boss, or counselor to ask them to write your recommendation paper.

Prepare For Interview @ Interview Questions from different fields  Sep 2022—Present

Most Korean Companies require 1-1 1-multiple interview. Knowing frameoworks of QUESTIONS will make you ease into the mood during the session. Also, don't forget to boost up your confidence! Korean Companies love to see your passion, interest, and commitment as an employee. If you didn't get in, no worries at all. There is high demand of foreign workers in recent Korean work industry. Find out the reason you didn't get accepted, and move on with those elements!
